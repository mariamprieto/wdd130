Site Name

River People Company

Site Purpose with Goals

The site purpose is to captivate groups of students from different schools to live a new
adventure through expeditions in different rivers with excellent prices and as a study tool for biology class.

Audience and Scenarios

The Audience for this website will be the directors and students of the different schools

The Scenarios are:

  1. Flash elements and videos:
    In this part include photos and videos of some previous expeditions so that visitors to the
    page can feel motivated to participate in this adventure and have fun while studying.
  2. Marketing promotions:
    Show price packages by number of students and seasons for school groups, including information
    on services, prices and the number of days to carry out the expedition.
  3. Educational information:
    In this scenario will contain educational information about rivers, nature, the use of rafting
    equipment, etc.
  4. Experiences from students:
    In this part we will have testimonials from students about their experience in expeditions with
    River People Company.
