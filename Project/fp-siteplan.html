Site Name

Collectsy Company

Purpose and Goals

The purpose of this website is to attract all companies with problems in the recovery of their income, due to late payments by their clients, in order to provide effective and efficient collection management through our platform.

Audience and Scenarios

The audience for this website will be companies from any industrial sector that have problems with the collection of their income.Potential audience will be companies that present the following characteristics: accounts receivable older than 60 days, few employees to manage collections and a wide portfolio of clients.
The scenarios are:
  1. Description of the service:
    This part will detail the service offered by this company.
  2. Subscription and collection management plan:
    This part will present the subscription form and present the collection management plans with their different rates.
  3. Updated news:
    This part will present the latest updates that are related to finance, economics and marketing that are helpful for client companies.
  4. Clients:
    In this scenario will be presented the clients that Collectsy owns and their testimonies regarding the quality of the service.
