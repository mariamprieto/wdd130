Importance of the Temples

Temples are literally houses of the LordLord.
They are holy places of worship where individuals make sacred covenants with God.

Why is the temple important?

  1. The primary purpose of the temple is to provide the ordinances
    necessary for our exaltation in the celestial kingdom.
  2. Temple blessings are as essential for each of us as was our baptism.
  3. Temple ordinances guide us to our Savior and give us the blessings
    that come to us through the Atonement of Jesus Christ.
  4. We are instructed in the scriptures to build temples and prepare our
    lives to be worthy to partake of the sacred temple ordinances and covenants.
