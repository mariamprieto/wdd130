Overview

Site Name

Collectsy Company

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to attract all companies with problems in the recovery of their income, due to late payments by their clients, in order to provide effective and efficient collection management through our platform.

Audience

The audience for this website will be companies from any industrial sector that have problems with the collection of their income.Potential audience will be companies that present the following characteristics: accounts receivable older than 60 days, few employees to manage collections and a wide portfolio of clients.

Scenerios

The scenarios are:

  • Description of the service:
    This part will detail the service offered by this company.
  • Subscription and collection management plan:
    This part will present the subscription form and present the collection management plans with their different rates.
  • Updated news:
    This part will present the latest updates that are related to finance, economics and marketing that are helpful for client companies.
  • Clients:
    In this scenario will be presented the clients that Collectsy owns and their testimonies regarding the quality of the service.

Typography

Heading Font: Alegreya Sans

Alegreya Sans is a humanist sans serif family with a calligraphic feeling that conveys a dynamic and varied rhythm. This gives a pleasant feeling to readers of long texts. This font was selected as it is eye-catching, modern, and highly readable.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

Open Sans was designed with an upright stress, open forms and a neutral, yet friendly appearance. It was optimized for print, web, and mobile interfaces, and has excellent legibility characteristics in its letterforms. This font was selected as it is easy to read and very legible.

About us

Collectsy is a company dedicated to the administration of collections for companies of all kinds that require regular collection from their clients.
Our platform allows your company to offer your customers the option of paying with automatic debit to their bank account, providing great comfort and peace of mind while your company ensures that customers stay up to date with their commitments.
