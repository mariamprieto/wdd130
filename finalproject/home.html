WHAT IS COLLECTSY?

Collectsy is a company dedicated to the administration of collections for companies of all kinds that require regular collection from their clients. Our platform allows your company to offer your customers the option of paying with automatic debit to their bank account, providing great comfort and peace of mind while your company ensures that customers stay up to date with their commitments. We are a team made up of professionals with experience in the area of sales, marketing and computer systems, committed to our clients with the highest values of quality and service.

SECURE YOUR COMPANY'S INCOME

With our system, you give your company the ability to ensure that your customers are kept up-to-date, thus generating regular cash flow and avoiding inadvertent customer arrears.
Usually companies allocate many resources to the collection management of their services. Serviace helps you avoid this problem by putting its entire platform at your service and being your ally in the collection processes.
